The Story

Discover Distinction: Our Legacy

The Kolinberg Philharmonic was founded in 1996 with a singular vision: to push the boundaries of classical music while honoring the masters.

Over nearly three decades, the orchestra has established itself globally, celebrated for its rich sound and fearless repertoire choices. The Philharmonic regularly engages in stellar collaborations, leading to acclaimed appearances at major international festivals, including the Mahler Festival in Amsterdam, and features on Decca Classics.

We create powerful, unforgettable experiences that inspire a new generation of music lovers, always upholding our commitment to Discover Distinction.
