28 Sep. 2025

Kolinberg Philharmonic Announces 2026 Asian Tour

We are thrilled to announce our upcoming tour across Asia in Spring 2026. The orchestra will visit Tokyo, Seoul, and Taipei, performing major works by Brahms and Tchaikovsky. Tickets go on sale to the general public on 15 October 2025.

12 Oct. 2025

Violinist Anne-Sophie Mutter Joins for Charity Gala

Legendary violinist Anne-Sophie Mutter returns to the Kolinberg stage for a special one-night Charity Gala. All proceeds will support our 'Music for All' education initiative. Join us for an evening of Mozart and Mendelssohn this December.
05 Nov. 2025

Young Conductor Fellowship Winner Announced for 2026

The Kolinberg Philharmonic is proud to announce Elias Weber as the winner of the 2026 Young Conductor Fellowship. Selected from over 200 applicants, Weber will mentor under Maestro James and conduct two community concerts next season.
